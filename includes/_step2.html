Once Bob gives the green light for the transaction to occur, this transaction is communicated out to the rest of the participants in the network. Because everyone shares a copy of the latest Blockchain ledger being used, they can check to make sure that the transaction is valid.

In other words, if Alice had tampered with Bob's blockchain ledger to incorrectly show that she had a cookie—when in reality she did not—other people in the network will be able to see that and they will reject the transaction.
